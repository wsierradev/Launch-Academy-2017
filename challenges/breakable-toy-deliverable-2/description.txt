Elevator Pitch

A repository of arcades with a list of games for each arcade.
Users can add arcades and add games to each arcade.
Arcades contain pertinent information like list of games and location
info.(Hours of operation, Whether it is open, etc.)
Users can search for arcades in their area and find out
out what games are there.
Users can click on a game in an an arcade and find out info about that game.
